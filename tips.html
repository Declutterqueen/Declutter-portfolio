Top 5 Decluttering Tips for Small Bedrooms

Simple strategies to create space, calm, and control.

  1. Use under-bed storage boxes. Store seasonal clothes or shoes to clear out closet space.
  2. Hang hooks behind doors. Great for bags, robes, or accessories.
  3. Fold clothes vertically. This makes everything visible in drawers — no more digging!
  4. Declutter one shelf at a time. Avoid overwhelm by working in small zones.
  5. Donate what you don’t use. If you haven’t used it in 6 months, let it go.

Start with one drawer today — even a 10-minute declutter can reset your space and your mood.
